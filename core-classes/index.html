Explore Our Core Themes

Fundamentals

Worship is a topic that has many facets. A basic understanding of what worship is, why we worship, and how we worship is essential to any worship leader or musician. These lessons cover a broad range of topics including modern worship, spontaneous worship, intimacy with the Lord and more.

Leadership

These lessons teach about the practical aspects of worship leading such as set preparation and building a team as well as the more complex issues such as spontaneous worship leading and hosting God's Presence. Experienced instructors share what they know about running a team and leading a congregation.

Community

Every worship leader, pastor and musician understands that relationships with the congregation and senior pastor is vital to the success of any worship team. This theme discusses developing a culture of worship in the and inviting stronger relationships amongst teams and leaders.

Lifestyle

Musical worship is only a small portion of what defines worship. Worship includes how we live our lives, how we treat our family, how we handle our work and how we love people around us. These lessons discuss the heart of worship that should evident in our daily lives.

Songwriting

Our leaders and writers from many different backgrounds share their tips, tricks and tools for successful songwriting. Learn how they find inspiration and how they capture that into melody and lyrics. These lessons empower you to move forward as a songwriter who can write for whatever genre you are passionate about.

Heart

It's important that all believers understand their God-given identity as sons and daughters of the King. These lessons focus on having a heart postioned rightly before God and knowing who you are as you approach His throne in worship.
