Main Sessions

Cultivate a deeper relationship with God and a greater understanding of worship

In WorshipU Main Sessions you'll hear a powerful selection of teachings on matters of the heart, the Spirit of God, the power of worship, and Biblical foundations of worship. With an emphasis on your personal journey with The Lord, these teachings provide valuable insights for all worshippers that wil nurture your leadership skills, relationships, and overall pursuit of God.

  • Jonathan Helser - The Heart Above the Gift
