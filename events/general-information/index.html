General Information

Age Limit
The age requirement to attend WorshipU On Campus is 18 and older. Exceptions may be considered for minor students attending with a parent/legal guardian and for students with a High School Diploma/GED.
Childcare
There is no childcare available for the WorshipU On Campus.
Housing
Each student is responsible for his or her own living expenses and arrangements. A housing link will be provided to accepted BSW students.
Information on the Redding Area:
You may contact the Chamber of Commerce at (530) 225-4433. For subscriptions to our local newspaper, the Record Searchlight, call (800) 666-2772 or (530) 243-2424 or visit www.redding.com or www.ci.redding.ca.us for more information.
Transportation
Airport - First Class Shuttle service is a shuttle service that operates 24/7 between the Sacramento Airport and Redding. For information and details go to www.firstclassshuttle.net . The Greyhound bus offers service between San Francisco Airport and Redding. For information and details go to www.greyhound.com .

Daily - As Bethel Church is not in walking distance from some residences, students may rent a car, purchase a used bike on craigslist ( www.redding.craigslist.org ), use the Redding Bus System (RABA - www.rabaride.com ), or rideshare with other students.
Auditing of Classes
If you are unable to enroll as a full-time attendee of Bethel School of Worship or would prefer to only attend a few classes, you may choose to AUDIT up to 5 days/$500. Auditor registration is every morning 9:00-10:00am at the Auditor Registration table in the foyer outside the sanctuary doors.

Auditing rates are as follows:
$100 - Full Day including night classes
$25 - Elective classes (Note: elective classes are based on availability. Enrolled students get priority scheduling. Availability will be determined at the time of payment.)
$25 - Morning Worship + Main Session Speaker
You may email bsw@ibethel.org for the Schedule of Classes (afternoon and elective classes limited to availability, please come prepared with your first and second choices for each session.)
