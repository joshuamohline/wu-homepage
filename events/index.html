Learn about WorshipU On Campus

WorshipU On-Campus is a worship school that consists of 4 major components. Main sessions teach on matter of the heart, the kingdom, and lifestyle. Breakout sessions are specific for instrument training, songwriting and worship leading. Extended time of praise and worship everyday from word renown leaders. Community activities make it easy to build friendships with like-minded people from around the globe.
