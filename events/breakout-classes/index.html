Breakout Classes

Hands on training for musicians, songwriters, worship leaders, and creative artists.

Breakout sessions offer you a chance to get hands on training on your instrument or skill of choice. Bethel Music musicians teach from experience on guitar, drums, bass, keyboards, and more. We also offer a large selection of skill development classes for photographers, sound engineers, painters, dancers, songwriters, and worship leaders. In the intimate setting of the breakout classes, you will be able to get a more personal connection to grow in your area of interest.

Voice:

In the voice class, we will be discussing the mechanics of the voice to help you find your true voice. We will be observing and evaluating common problem areas and negative tendencies that people have while singing, such as pushing, pulling, squeezing, over-blowing, lack of strength and singing throaty, nasally or breathy. We will then discuss and implement practical ways to correct these things.

Private lessons will be available from the instructor for an additional fee. You may go to www.facebook.com/TrueVoxLessons or bookfresh.com/service/redding/truevox/499633617 to schedule a private lesson
