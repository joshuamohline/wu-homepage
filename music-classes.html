Explore Our Music Classes

Music Theory

Learn the foundations of music with this course on music theory. Lessons teach on scales, the number system and building chords. Classes emphasize the simplicity of music as it applies to the worship setting.

Vocals

Learn the basics of vocal health, technique and airflow. Lessons teach on how to properly warm up and how to maximize your vocal skills. Classes emphasize the importance understanding the instrument of the voice and what it's capable of.

Drums

Learn the basics of drumming and the application of drum skills to the worship environment. Lessons teach on rudiments, mic placement, playing parts and general technique. Classes focus on using the drums as an instrument to enhance worship.

Bass

Learn the fundamentals of bass guitar as it pertains to the worship setting. Lessons teach on bass finger technique, intonation, tone and setting up your guitar and rig. Tips on gear and amp settings help bass players of any skill level sound great, wherever they are.

Piano

Learn the fundamentals of playing the piano. Classes teach on scales, notes, major and minor chords as well as incorporating style and tone into the worship setting. Lessons will empower any beginning or advanced player to sound great while contributing to any worship team.

Keyboards

Learn the basics of midi, Ableton Live and Reason. Lessons will teach on how to create an efficient set up for the live worship setting. These classes intend on teaching you how to run creative and effective loops, sounds, pads and synths in worship.

Electric

Learn the basics of electric guitar and move towards advanced techniques. Lessons on tone, amps, pedals, scales and technique aim to give guitarists a well rounded perspective on applying the electric guitar to the worship setting.

Acoustic Guitar

Learn the acoustic guitar starting from the basics and moving into advanced skills. Lessons teach on major and minor chords, scales, stringing the guitar, alternate tunings, capos and more. Watch the lessons in order or skip around depending on your preference.
