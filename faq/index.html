Frequently Asked Questions

When can I enroll for WorshipU?

You can sign up for WorshipU at anytime! Once you create an account, you can immediately start watching any video lesson in the program.

How do I pay for WorshipU?

The basic WorshipU course is $24.95/month, however, we occassionaly run enrollment specials that allow students to sign up at a lower monthly rate. These rates are subject to change. Once you sign up, you will get the first 2 weeks for free, as a part of your trial. After that, your credit card on file will automatically charge for your first month of access and will be charged every subsequent month on the same day of the month as the first charge.

How do I cancel my subscription?

We hope that you don't want to cancel your subscription, but you can do so at any time by going into your account settings and selecting "Cancel Subscription". However, you may also pause your subscription by selecting "Pause Subscription". Choosing this option will allow you to keep all your profile information and account activity attached to your account, so it's saved for you whenever you resume your subscription.

What do I get when I sign up for WorshipU?

When you sign up, you will immediately have access to over 200 HD video lessons covering topics including, but not limited to: leadership, character, community, team building, instrument training, Biblical foundations, and more. New lessons are released every week so that you never run out of great content to empower you or your team.
You will have access to every video in our archive, regardless of your instrument or role.

Is WorshipU a school?

WorshipU is an online training program that exists to equip and empower worship leaders, musicians and worshippers to live transformed. The classes are not accredited, however, they provide a comprehensive set of teachings that will benefit worshippers of every skill level and experience.

What are the system requirements for my computer?

WorshipU content is supported on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, IOS Safari, Mobile Chrome, iPhone and Android. WorshipU will NOT work on Internet Explorer. Depending on your device, it may also work on Windows Phone and Blackberry.

Do I have to watch the lessons at a specific time of day?

All lessons are pre-recorded and will be released at the start of each week. Once a lesson is in the archive, you can watch it whenever you want, as many times as you want.

Interviews with our instructors will take place periodically and will be released after they have been recorded. This is where you can submit any question you like and have the opportunity for our instructors to answer them in the interview.

What kind of time commitment is WorshipU?

The more time you invest into WorshipU, the more you will get out of it. However, there is no minimum obligation. You can engage discussion boards, music lessons and core classes as much as you would like. We hope to provide enough new videos every week that you never run out of great content, no matter how much time you put in.
Our average user spends about 3-5 hours in WorshipU each week.

Do you have group rates?

We have group rates! Please contact our support team at hello@worshipu.com to inquire.

How often are new videos released?

We will release at least 1 new video every week.

How do I change my credit card information?

You can update your payment information in the "Account Settings" tab of your profile.
